Return and Refund Policy
If you find that the shipped product doesn't work as described within our 7-day money-back guarantee period, we will be happy to issue you an exchange or full refund and pay for the return shipping of the unit.

If the item is damaged due to improper installation or other customer error, return and refund will not be accepted.
Item returned must be labelled with a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) Number.
Item returned without RMA# number will not be processed, so please contact us before returning.
Replacement or full refund will be made for defective products returned after seller's verification.
